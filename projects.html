PROJECTS

Project Title:

  • Comprehensive Analysis Detection Of Credit Card Fraud Through ML
Technology used in project:
  • Language : Python Using Machine Learning
Project Description:
  • In The World Of Finance, As Technology Grows, New Systems Of Business Making Come Into Picture. Credit Card System Is One Among Them. But Because Of A Lot Of Loopholes In This System, A Lot Of Problems Are Aroused In This System In The Method Of Credit Card Scams. Due To This The Industry And Customers Who Are Using Credit Cards Are Facing A Huge Loss. There Is A Deficiency Of Investigation Lessons On Examining Practical Credit Card Figures In Arrears To Privacy Issues. Fraud Detection In Credit Cards Using Decision Tree Algorithm Which Is Utilized For Both Classification And Regression.
